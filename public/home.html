How it works

Three simple steps from setup to automated attendance.

🎤

1. Enrol Voice

Students register by recording a short voice sample. The system creates a unique voiceprint for each student.

📍

2. Verify Location

At class time, the student's GPS is checked. They must be within 50 m of the classroom before voice check-in is unlocked.

📈

3. Analyse

View instant reports, identify at-risk students, and export data for institutional records.
